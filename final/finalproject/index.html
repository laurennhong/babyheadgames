WHICH TWIN WILL GET TO 30 POINTS FIRST?

A twin is chosen randomly. She will roll the dice and keep rolling. The score will continue to add up, unless she rolls a "one". Then the turn switches to the other twin. After each roll, you can either roll again, or you can pass to the other twin. The first twin to get 30 points or higher wins.

Oh, and if you roll two 1's, AKA snake eyes, your current score turns to 0!
